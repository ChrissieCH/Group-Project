Chargrilled salmon fajitas

A super speedy weeknight supper that takes just 15 minutes to make!


Ingredients

    4 salmon fillets
    Sunflower oil
    1 pack of tortillas
    2 avocados
    2 limes
    fajita spice mix
    salsa mix

Step 1

Coat the salmon in 1 tbsp of oil and add fajita spice mix.

Step 2

Add 1 tbsp oil to a frying pan and fry for 8 minutes until the salmon is charred.

Step 3

Mash the avocados with a fork, season and squeeze over the juice of 1 lime.

Step 4

Serve the salmon in large flakes over the tortillas, avocado, salsa and the other lime, cut into wedges.
